Happy Birthday ✦
written for you, on your special day

My love,

It’s your birthday — and of all the days in the year, this is the one I most want to get right. So I’m putting it into words, properly, the way I don’t always manage to say out loud.

I love you more than you could ever imagine. I know there are days you think I don’t love you as much as you love me — but deep down, I know the truth. What I feel for you runs so much deeper than I have ever known how to show.

We’ve had our ups and our downs. You met me at my worst — the unfinished, rougher version of me — and you stayed anyway. Everything good in who I’ve become, I owe in some part to you. You made me into the person I am today.

I know I’m not perfect. I’m still on the path to becoming the man I want to be for you — still learning, still growing. All I ask is that you walk that road with me. Stay, through the becoming and through all of it, and let’s give ourselves our happily ever after.

♥

and a few of the things I love about you

  • The way you stayed, even when I was at my very worst.
  • How you see the good in me long before I can see it myself.
  • Your laugh — the one that makes even the heaviest day feel lighter.
  • The quiet, stubborn way you never give up on us.

So on your birthday, this is my wish — not for me, but for us: more years, more ordinary days, more of you and me. Thank you for loving me through it all. Happy birthday, my love. Here’s to forever.

Forever yours,
— me

P.S. You are, and have always been, more than enough. Read this again any time you forget how deeply you are loved.
